What is a network?

A network is formed when more than two computer systems are
linked together. The size of a network can be as small as a handful of computers to millions (such as the internet). The computers in a network can share resources with each other, communicate, exchange files and more. LAN (Local Area Network) and WAN (Wide Area Network) are the most widespreaded types of networks. Furthermore, for a network to form, computers need the support of network devices (e.g. modem and
router). There are many sorts of network devices.
