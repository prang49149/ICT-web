How to protect your computer from viruses

Running antivirus software in the background on a computer will constanly check for virus attacks. Although various types of antivirus software work in different ways they have many common features. Such as comparing a possible virus with known viruses in their database, etc.

One way to help prevent virus attacls when accessing websites on the internet is to avoid unkown or suspicious-looking websites. If in doubt, don't access the website 1-2 look for security indicators such as https or the padlock symbol.

Also look out for odd behaviour in the URL when accessing a new website, for example from an advert in email, copy and paste the URL into the address bar at the top of the page rather than just cliking on the link in the email. This can help to avoid links to bogus/fake websites.

It is also not advisable to open emails (or any attachments) from unknown sources. Essentially, the best form of defence against malicious behavior when making use of the internet is to apply common sense.
